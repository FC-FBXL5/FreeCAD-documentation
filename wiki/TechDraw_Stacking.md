# TechDraw Stacking

## Description

The Stack Up <img alt="" src=images/TechDraw_StackUp.svg  style="width   *24px;">, Stack Down <img alt="" src=images/TechDraw_StackDown.svg  style="width   *24px;">, Stack Top <img alt="" src=images/TechDraw_StackTop.svg  style="width   *24px;"> and Stack Bottom <img alt="" src=images/TechDraw_StackBottom.svg  style="width   *24px;"> tools change the apparent depth of views on the page.

## Usage

-   Select a view in the page or tree.
-   Press the one of the stacking buttons, such as **<img src="images/TechDraw_StackTop.svg" width=16px> [Stack Top](TechDraw_Stacking.md)**.
-   Select one of the **TechDraw → <img src="images/TechDraw_StackTop.svg" width=16px> Stack Top** stacking options from the menu.

## Properties

-    **StackOrder**   * Over or under lap relative to other views.

## Scripting


**See also   ***

[TechDraw API](TechDraw_API.md) and [FreeCAD Scripting Basics](FreeCAD_Scripting_Basics.md).

Stacking order can be changed in scripts by changing the StackOrder property of a View\'s ViewObject.
