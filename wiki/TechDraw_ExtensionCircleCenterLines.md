# TechDraw ExtensionCircleCenterLines

## Description

The <img alt="" src=images/TechDraw_ExtensionCircleCenterLines.svg  style="width:24px;"> **Circle Center Lines** tool adds center lines to circles and arcs.

 <img alt="" src=images/TechDraw_ExtensionCircleCenterLinesExample.png  style="width:200px;">  
*On the right a circle with center lines*

## Usage

1.  Select one or more circles or arcs.
2.  Press the **<img src="images/TechDraw_ExtensionCircleCenterLines.svg" width=16px> [Circle Center Lines](TechDraw_ExtensionCircleCenterLines.md)** button.
3.  Center lines are added to each circle or arc.
