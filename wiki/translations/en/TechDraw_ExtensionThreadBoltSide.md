# TechDraw ExtensionThreadBoltSide/en

## Description

The <img alt="" src=images/TechDraw_ExtensionThreadBoltSide.svg  style="width:24px;"> **Thread section Shaft** tool adds a symbolic thread to the side view of a bolt.

<img alt="" src=images/TechDraw_ExtensionThreadBoltSideExample.png  style="width:400px;"> 
*On the right a bolt with a symbolic thread*

## Usage

1.  Select the two parallel lines of a bolt.
2.  There are several ways to invoke the tool:
    -   Press the **<img src="images/TechDraw_ExtensionThreadBoltSide.svg" width=16px> [Thread section Shaft](TechDraw_ExtensionThreadBoltSide.md)** button.
    -   Select the **TechDraw → Extensions: centerlines and threading → <img src="images/TechDraw_ExtensionThreadBoltSide.svg" width=16px> Cosmetic thread bolt side view** option from the menu.
3.  A symbolic thread is added.
