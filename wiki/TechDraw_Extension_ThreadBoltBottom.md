## Description

The <img alt="" src=images/TechDraw_ExtensionThreadBoltBottom.svg  style="width:24px;"> **Thread Bolt Bottom** tool creates symbolic threads at bolts.

 <img alt="" src=images/TechDraw_ExtensionThreadBoltBottomExample.png  style="width:200px;">  
*On the right a symbolic thread*

## Usage

1.  Select one or more circles representing bolts.
2.  Press the **<img src="images/TechDraw_ExtensionThreadBoltBottom.svg" width=16px> [Thread Bolt Bottom](TechDraw_Dimension_Horizontal.md)** button.
3.  Symbolic threads are added.
