# TechDraw ExtensionHoleCircle

## Description

The <img alt="" src=images/TechDraw_ExtensionHoleCircle.svg  style="width:24px;"> **Hole/Bolt Circle Centerlines** tool adds center lines to a hole/bolt circle.

 <img alt="" src=images/TechDraw_ExtensionHoleCircleExample.png  style="width:600px;">  
*On the right a hole/bolt circle with center lines*

## Usage

1.  Select at least three (or more) circles representing holes/bolts.
2.  Press the **<img src="images/TechDraw_ExtensionHoleCircle.svg" width=16px> [Hole/Bolt Circle Centerlines](TechDraw_ExtensionHoleCircle.md)** button.
3.  The centerlines are added.
